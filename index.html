DS3 DataHacks 2025

DataHacks 2025 was a Hackathon hosted by UCSD's Data Science Student Society (DS3). In a team of two others, my friends Teo and Arnav, we fed Pokemon Soundtracks and their real world counterparts into the Audio Spectrogram Transformer (AST) to see how much the composers derived from the real world areas the games are based on. I compiled all the soundtracks and music, as well as frontend using streamlit to present our project. You can check out the project with the link below!
