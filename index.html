Incessant

We created Incessant during a 48-hour game jam, with the theme "On Repeat". We leaned into the idea of repetition by creating a game that reflects the monotony of office life, with each in-game day presenting the same routine, with a new task layered in daily. I worked on both the programming and art alongside Teo who contributed to the music and art as well as Arnav who contributed to the programming. You can check out our game below!
